Characters

The core gameplay loop is doing favors for and befriending various people through smart dialogue choices and WarioWare-styled minigame tasks. Every single character can become Minty's friend.


Minty

A huge fanatic for all things anime, Minty discovers her destiny as the world's savior after a fateful encounter at the local shopping mall.


Lychee

An overwhelmingly gung-ho girl who's always on the lookout for new friends. She's never seen worn out.


Wes

Wes is a conceited socialite with a comically dramatic disdain for the tacky.


Kim

Easily drained and annoyed by the presence of others, Kim is an athlete who openly prefers solo workouts.


Bonnie

A pleasant mall-goer who never skips an opportunity to spend time with her two besties. Secretly a bit of a neat freak.


Jayla

A well-meaning, muscular woman who reads and repeats way too many inspirational poster quotes. Tends to bug Kim whenever they are both at the gym at the same time.


Mano

A lazy prankster who works at the local mall's guest service desk. Sometimes, she does actually do her job.

Locations

Mintyverse primarily takes place in a city known as Asteropolis. Characters can be found in specific locations, but may move around over the course of the game. Each area also has various challenges for Minty to overcome (though, they are only considered "challenges" because she isn't used to being out of the house...)

Minty's House

Minty lives with her parents and younger brother in the metro area. The house is already a bit too big for the family, but her brother's absence for school has made it feel all the more empty. The house itself is pretty plain, as one would expect with a cookie-cutter suburban home.
Characters present: Minty's mom, Minty's dad

Pine Blossom Shopping Mall

Asteropolis' local mall - a solid 25 minute bus ride from Minty's house, which makes it just inconvenient enough for regular trips. With a plethora of stores and activities, it makes a popular site for patrons of all ages and interests. Pine trees don't actually have blossoms.
Characters present: Lychee, Bonnie, Mai, Sugar, Mano, Ash

YO-LO Wellness Center

The YO-LO Wellness Center houses an overwhelming amount of exercise equipment, spaces for various athletic activities and a snack shop. Interestingly enough, there is a robotics club that meets in one of the multipurpose rooms, but it's relatively small due to most prospective members being too intimidated to pass through the gym to get there.
Characters present: Kim, Jayla, Nell, Hammy, Ika

Clout Nine

Open all hours of the night, Clout Nine is the center of all night life in Asteropolis. It's also open during the daytime, but there isn't much to do there beyond awkwardly ordering a cocktail at 2pm. They're currently trying to expand their menu and event schedule to facilitate "day life" profit, as a healthy capitalist would.
Characters present: Wes, Danyel, Aria, Matt

University of Asten

At an economically convenient distance from many downtown amenities is the state university. The University of Asten boasts a large enrollment and graduation rate and a wide variety of programs. Minty's brother, Fern, attends this school for pre-med. Minty herself attended this school for computer science, but has yet to do anything meaningful with the degree.
Characters present: Fern, Diego, Huynh, Luck, Amy
