Stuff I'm working on!!

Ideally I'll have subpages on this website for these, notably the non-BftB stuff (BftB is already being documented on Notion and I'm too lazy to crosspost). It's not the greatest priority in the world, though.

Bolt from the Blue

Rotating in my head at a constant velocity is my silly worldbuilding/gamedev project. Inspired by silly classic JRPGs about little guys going on big adventures.

Mintyverse

What was originally supposed to be a "break" from the heavy worldbuilding of BftB gradually became a bit of a reflection on my adolescence, weirdly enough. It's a really lame story about a chuuni determined to save the world from its inevitable end. I plan on turning this into a game as well, albeit a much smaller one than BftB. There's also a TOYHOU.SE folder for this, but it's a giant WIP.

Deep Sea Artscape

Just a Discord server I spend way too much time polishing + managing (I say this lovingly. The people in there make it worth going this hard for). It's designed with OC/worldbuilding/projectdev in mind, so there's channels for people's projects, monthly events/prompts, etc.
