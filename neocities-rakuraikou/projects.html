Bolt from the Blue

Rotating in my head at a constant velocity is my silly worldbuilding/gamedev project. Inspired by silly classic JRPGs about little guys going on big adventures. There is a TOYHOU.SE folder for this project that I'm currently working on.

Mintyverse

What was originally supposed to be a "break" from the heavy worldbuilding of BftB gradually became a bit of a reflection on my adolescence, weirdly enough. It's a really lame story about a chuuni determined to save the world from its inevitable end. I plan on turning this into a game as well, albeit a much smaller one than BftB. There's also a TOYHOU.SE folder for this, but it's also a big WIP lols.

Deep Sea Artscape

Just a Discord server I spend way too much time polishing + managing (I say this lovingly. The people in there make it worth going this hard for). It's designed with OC/worldbuilding/projectdev in mind, so there's channels for people's projects, monthly events/prompts, etc.

Chalk For Bones

It's just a personal writing collection. Poetry, letters, prose... Whatever lols. Any necessary content warnings will be noted on the page itself.
