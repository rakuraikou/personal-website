She's really something...

It's all so embarrassing to think about, let alone write down, but at least I can get it out here. That way I won't have to embarrass myself anywhere else.

It feels like every day I'm finding new things to like about her, even if every day she is just the same as she was the day before. How is that possible? She's always found new ways to surprise me, yet she doesn't seem to try at all.
