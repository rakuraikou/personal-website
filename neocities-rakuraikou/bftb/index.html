Walking into San Ven...

The evening breeze is cool but comforting. It catches you at just the right angle, soothing your skin and keeping hair out of your eyes. This is certainly the best weather that San Ven has to offer, and you fully intend on making the most of it on your visit.

A few of the city's highlights await you, and you stop to decide which sight you'd like to visit first.

  • Just down the path is Eliteca's campus, where a grand library houses complete knowledge of Luxos, the realm of light.
  • Castle Cieliso towers over the highest point in the city. It's the perfect place to familiarize yourself the most important and notable figures.
  • If you're looking to hear adventurer's tales, look no further than the Skysea Port to satisfy your thirst for thrills.
  • Finally, you may find any miscellaneous curiosities satisfied in the busy and ever-evolving Tradewinds Marketplace.
