RAIDEN

Raiden is an optimistic, cheerful individual whose kind heart knows no limits. She will always extend a helping hand to anyone in need, even if they don’t really deserve it. Though she can appear rather laidback at times, Raiden always knows when to really step up to a challenge. While a bit naïve, her story and experiences are living proof that kind energy can go a long way.

ARACELI

Aside from her inability to properly control her own aspect, Araceli is the optimal heir to the Cielicano throne. She deeply embraces this, but everything changes when she discovers her father's true nature and hidden intentions with her castle friend, Raiden. On impulse, she takes Raiden and escapes the castle. Araceli’s goal now is to take this opportunity to understand the world of Luxos that already knows her all too well.

SAFFRON

The second-born son of House Infiaco, Saffron, is met with a plethora of noble-related duties to his land - none of which involving becoming a ruler himself. Despite this, he strives to prove himself as a worthy successor to his family and people. With motivation from his easygoing older brother, Saffron decides that the best way to fairly earn the title of Head of the House is to obtain the favor of the Champion of Fire.

COLE

Cole’s reputation as the renegade first mate of arguably the most notorious band of sky pirates precedes her; few individuals are comfortable approaching her for any reason, and she prefers it that way. However, under all of that tough skin, Cole hides a tragic secret that floods her with guilt. When Raiden, Araceli and Saffron request a means of travel, Cole is forced to face this buried trauma head-on.
