Inspirations

Behind every "very bad but very good" RPG is an "even worse but even better" RPG.
Bolt from the Blue is meant to emulate the untitled subgenre of JRPGs that try to be both modern (by flipping the JRPG formula either mechanically or narratively) and "classic" (to appeal to golden age nostalgia)
  • Bravely series (esp. Bravely Default: Flying Fairy)
  • Voice of Cards series (esp. The Forsaken Maiden)
  • Black Clover
  • The Legend of Zelda series (esp. WW/BOTW/TOTK)
  • Kingdom Hearts
  • Octopath Traveler
  • Genshin Impact
  • Naruto
