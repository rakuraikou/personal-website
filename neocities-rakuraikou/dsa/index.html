What is Deep Sea Artscape?

I'm currently working on transferring the resources on this page (particularly the event page stuff)! Images may be broken for the time being. Deep Sea Artscape (DSA) is an art Discord server specifically centered around OC (original character) and original worldbuilding discussion. The server aims to create a fun and friendly sea-themed space to encourage creators to work on and share their projects, as well as bring together like-minded artists to talk about original characters and stories.

I (Len) started DSA in February 2022 as a corner for some original character roleplay friends to hang out together after the roleplay group fizzled out. It's since grown into something much larger, and I feel like I'm very fortunate to run such an awesome community!
