~4 years ago

I have such mixed feelings about Raiden. It's a little annoying, the way she tails me and asks me what I need. I get it's her job, but it almost feels like pity at this point. She knows what I want - I want her to do whatever it was she did at the meadow so that I can use my magic. Nothing matters more to me than being able to protect my people.

At the same time, however, I appreciate the attention to my needs. The only time she asks what she can help with is when I actually need help with something, and I'm not sure how she cues into it so well. Maybe following me around all day trained her intuition?

She's not just helpful though... Raiden's always smiling and saying nice things to me, and it makes me feel so...relaxed? That isn't the word. I mean, she's always been this way, and I've always felt comforted by her, even when I'm annoyed. And she's really quite strong and skillful, too...

Well, I really don't have the time to think about this! Not with all of my responsibilities, anyway.
