The Windknights brought someone to the castle yesterday, she's a girl like me and we're the same age (I think). My father tells me she is all by herself and the Windknights are looking for her family. I hope they can find them soon because she's really nice!! She says her name is Raiden, but she doesn't know anything else about herself. That's so weird??

My father says that as long as it doesn't get in the way of church visits and training, I can talk with Raiden. He says that Lady Catherine said that the most important thing is growing closer with Loni. I think he's right. Going to the church is a lot of work, but it will make me very strong when I grow up!! I'm the princess after all!!

Also, I have been spending a lot of time with Roga. She has been taking me to the market after training to buy books. I like to read big adventure books!!! Roga says that she'll buy for whatever I want to read, isn't that so sweet?? Raiden comes along sometimes too, but she's not really interested in books. She does help carry them which is so nice!
