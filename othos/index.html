Octopath Traveler: House of Stars

"Embark on an adventure all your own. Where will you go? What will you do? Whose tale will you bring to life? Every path is yours to take."

Octopath Traveler: House of Stars is my Octopath OC fan project! I really enjoyed playing Octopath Traveler and Octopath Traveler II, and wanted to try my hand at building a world with the framework established by the games. I have no intention of making OT:HOS into an actual game, but I have been enjoying making art and writing for the characters and their journeys!
